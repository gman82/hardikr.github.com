Yesterday, I got this e-mail from Flipkart:

Hello,

We recently sent you an Availability notification for the book "Liar's Poker: The Great Powers, Yugoslavia And The Wars Of The Future"  (http://www.flipkart.com/liar-poker-michel-collon-great-book-0965691667) as earlier requested by you on December 14, 2010.

Regrettably, the notification was due to an error at our end.  The issue was fixed earlier today and needless to say, will not be repeated. Our apologies if we got your hopes up with regards to a book that you had long been waiting for. Be assured, we will continue to maintain a record of your request(s). The next time we send you an Availability email, you can be sure the book will be available for ordering.

Sincere apologies again from all of us at Flipkart.com. Do let us know if we can be of any further assistance.

Regards,
Flipkart Customer Support Team

I was left open-mouthed when I read this ! They're actually sending me an e-mail apologizing for getting my hopes up! Apart from providing wonderful books which I could never otherwise find in India, they have this sort of customer support.

This, is why Flipkart rocks. Kudos! I think you're poised for a hell of an exit when Amazon enters India and acquires you guys.
